The Durand Clinic treats the root cause, not just the behavior.

Trauma & PTSD

For individuals carrying complex trauma, childhood adversity, acute life disruption, or occupational exposure. Equine-assisted psychotherapy is the core modality. Horses are prey animals with extraordinary sensitivity to human emotion and nervous system states — they mirror what the participant carries, facilitating processing that talk therapy alone cannot reach.

For high-performing individuals whose health has been sacrificed to achievement. Post-Acute Integration

For individuals completing treatment at another facility who need a supported, nature-based transition period before returning to daily life. Continued clinical support in a non-institutional environment. The bridge between structured care and the real world.

Core Modality

Equine-Assisted Psychotherapy

Horses are prey animals — extraordinarily attuned to human emotion, body language, and nervous system states. They don’t judge. They don’t perform. They mirror what the participant carries, in real time, creating breakthroughs that traditional talk therapy cannot reach alone.

Combined with nature-based challenges, somatic therapies, and a clinical team that treats the whole person — not just the presenting behavior — equine-assisted psychotherapy is the foundation of every residential program delivered through The Durand Clinic.

32-Day Structure

From Stabilization to Integration

Every program follows a carefully designed arc — the nervous system settles before deeper work begins.

Week 1

Stabilization

Comprehensive health evaluation at The Durand Clinic’s clinical facility. Introduction to the horses and the property. Establishing daily rhythm. The nervous system settles.

Weeks 2–3

Deep Work

Intensive equine-assisted psychotherapy. Nature-based challenges increase. Counseling sessions go deeper. Physical training builds strength and confidence. Somatic therapies support the body.

Week 4

Integration

Trigger mapping and prevention planning. Digital health platform configured for ongoing monitoring. Aftercare schedule established. Discharge plan built collaboratively.

Ongoing

Aftercare

Virtual check-in within 7 days. Monthly medical review. Quarterly psychotherapy sessions. Continuous monitoring through The Durand Clinic’s digital platform. Return stays available.
