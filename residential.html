The 32 Days

Arriving. Going Deeper. Looking Forward.

Every program follows a carefully designed arc. The body and mind settle before deeper work begins.

Week 1

Arriving

Comprehensive health assessment at The Durand Clinic’s clinical facility. Introduction to the horses and the property. Establishing a daily rhythm. The body and mind begin to settle in an environment designed for exactly that.

Weeks 2–3

Going Deeper

Equine sessions intensify. Conversations go to harder places. Physical strength builds alongside emotional resilience. Somatic therapies support the body through the process. After Week 3, guided outings may be introduced.

Week 4

Looking Forward

What does life look like from here? Mapping the landscape ahead. Building practical skills for navigating it. The Durand Clinic’s digital health platform is configured for ongoing connection. A plan built collaboratively.

Ongoing

Staying Connected

A call within 7 days. Monthly check-ins with the medical director. Quarterly sessions with the psychotherapist. Continuous monitoring. The option to return whenever it would be helpful. Annual membership available.
