How It Works

Healing Through Balance & Flow

TCM operates on the principle that the body contains channels or meridians that allow energy to flow through blood, nerves, and tissues. When this flow is disrupted or stagnated, health issues arise. Our practitioners combine acupuncture, cupping, moxibustion, and herbal remedies to restore energy flow and whole-body health.

Every appointment begins with a detailed patient history and thorough examination — a calming, relaxing, and enlightening experience designed around you.
