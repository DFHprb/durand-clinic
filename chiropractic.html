Treatment Methods

A Full Range of Techniques

We combine proven manual therapies, exercise-based rehabilitation, and advanced modalities to deliver lasting results — not just temporary relief.

Diversified Spinal Manipulation

Controlled joint mobilization to restore proper alignment, reduce pain, and improve nervous system function.

Soft Tissue Techniques

Manual therapy targeting muscles and connective tissue to relieve tension, reduce inflammation, and restore movement.

Exercise Prescription

Personalized strength, balance, and flexibility training programs to support your recovery and prevent re-injury.

Stretching

Targeted stretching protocols to restore muscle relaxation and full joint mobility as part of your treatment plan.

Acupuncture

Used to restore neuromuscular function, reduce pain signals, and support the body’s natural healing response.

Kinesiotaping™

Specialized taping techniques to correct movement patterns, offload injured tissue, and reduce pain during activity.

Myofascial Cupping Therapy

Promotes blood flow, breaks up fascial adhesions, and accelerates tissue healing in chronic or acute conditions.
