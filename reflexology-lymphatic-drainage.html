Treatment Methods

Two Therapies. One Integrated Session.

Whether you book reflexology, lymphatic drainage, or both, each treatment is tailored to your body’s needs on the day — gentle, deeply effective, and restorative.

Foot Reflexology

Beginning with gentle stretches and warm-up movements, your therapist systematically works each reflex point on the feet — corresponding to organs and body systems — finishing with a relaxing massage using your choice of professional aromatherapy essential oils.

Hand Reflexology

Using specialized techniques on the hands, your therapist massages the corresponding reflex points to stimulate and balance each organ and system in the body — delivering the same therapeutic benefits as foot reflexology through precise hand work.

Lymphatic Drainage Massage

Using lighter, rhythmic pressure along the body’s lymphatic map — including the legs, back, face, feet, arms, and abdomen — this specialized massage stimulates lymph flow, reduces swelling and water retention, and strengthens immune system function.

Aromatherapy Integration

All reflexology treatments incorporate professional aromatherapy essential oil blends selected by you — enhancing the therapeutic effect and deepening relaxation through the synergy of scent and touch.

Zone Therapy

Rooted in the principle that the body is divided into ten vertical zones, reflexology releases blockages within these pathways to increase circulation across the digestive, respiratory, reproductive, muscular, nervous, and immune systems.

Customized to You

You recline comfortably with pillows, warm blankets, and optional eye covers. Each session is fully adapted to your changing needs and preferences — adjusting pressure, focus areas, and techniques as your body requires.
