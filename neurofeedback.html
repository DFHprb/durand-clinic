How It Works

Real-Time Brainwave Self-Regulation

During a session, sensors placed on your scalp monitor your brain's electrical activity in real time. When NeurOptimal® detects suboptimal brainwave patterns, it delivers a subtle pause in the audio you’re listening to — a gentle signal that prompts your brain to self-correct and operate more efficiently.

No conscious effort is required. Your brain does the work naturally. Most clients notice meaningful benefits within several sessions, with approximately 30 sessions recommended for lasting results.
