Treatment Methods

A Full Range of Techniques

Our registered massage therapists draw on a broad toolkit of evidence-informed modalities, tailored to your specific needs and health goals.

Swedish Massage

Circulation-focused technique using kneading, long strokes, and circular movements to relax muscles and improve overall wellbeing.

Lymphatic Drainage

Light pressure therapy that encourages the natural drainage of lymph fluid, removing waste and toxins from body tissue.

Fascial Stretch Therapy

Practitioner-assisted stretching that targets the joint capsule and fascia, improving mobility, reducing pain, and enhancing performance.

Prenatal & Postnatal Massage

Specialized massage during and after pregnancy to ease discomfort, reduce swelling, relieve tension, and support recovery.

Deep Tissue Therapy

Sustained pressure targeting the inner layers of muscle and connective tissue to break up scar tissue and relieve chronic tension.

Trigger Point Therapy

Targeted treatment of hyperirritable muscle knots that cause local and referred pain, restoring normal muscle function.
