Treatment Methods

Evidence-Based Techniques

Our physiotherapists draw on a full range of evidence-based modalities, tailored to your specific condition, goals, and recovery timeline.

  • ✓ Manual Therapy — guided joint movement to restore function
  • ✓ Therapeutic Exercises — strength, balance, and flexibility training
  • ✓ Stretching Techniques — muscle relaxation and joint mobility
  • ✓ Patient Education — understanding injury mechanisms and prevention
  • ✓ Myofascial Cupping — blood flow and tissue healing
  • ✓ TENS Therapy — low-voltage electrical stimulation for pain relief
  • ✓ Kinesiotaping™ — posture correction and swelling reduction
